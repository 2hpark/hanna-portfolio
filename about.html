Who Am I?

My name is Hanna, Im a second year student at the University of Waterloo studying Global Business and Digital Arts.

What are my Inspirations?

Movies have always served as a big inspiration for me. I also admire the artwork from various anime, manga, and video games, and most lately, music and fashion have had a major influence in my taste!

Why Design?

The feeling I got as a kid when I watched or saw an amazing piece of media inspired me to create art myself. I’ve always practiced traditional art growing up, but digital tools like after effects and procreate opened up a whole new world to me. Since then, I’ve been on a journey to create media that gives others the same feeling of awe that I felt as a kid.
